## 🎀 What is UWU Languaged?

**UWU Languaged** is a feature-packed text-modding tool, built by me for the pure fun of it. Whether you want to "uwu-ify" your messages or just mess around with text, this app has you covered.

Designed to be **as fun as possible**, it includes a variety of features and options that let you customize your text experience to your heart's content. 

### ✨ Features
* **Automatic Conversion**: It makes 'r' into 'w' and 'l' into 'w'.
* **Emoji Swapping**: :) becomes >w< and :( becomes >m<.
* **Customization**: Not happy with the default settings? You can tell it exactly what to look for and what to replace it with!

### 🛠️ How It Works
**UWU Languaged** uses a powerful process called **Regex (Regular Expression) Replacement** to handle text transformations in real-time:

1.  **The Scanner**: When you type text, the app scans through every word, looking for specific patterns you've defined.
2.  **The Engine**: It uses a lookup table to instantly convert letters like 'r' and 'l' into 'w'. For emojis like `:)` or `:(`, the app uses **Regex** to find those specific punctuation patterns and swap them out for your custom `>w<` or `>m<` strings instantly.
3.  **The Custom Engine**: You aren't limited to my choices. Because I built a **Custom Replacement Table**, you can add your own "Find" and "Replace" rules. Every time you process text, the app checks your custom list first, giving you total control over the output.
